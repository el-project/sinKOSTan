What is sinKOSTan

sinKOStan is a small furniture business that provides boarding house children's goods that are unique. make the room more comfortable and eye-catching.

History

the city of yogyakarta has the nickname "student city", where many students from outside the region come to study at the high school to university level. so that many students choose to boarding and find it difficult to meet the needs of the items needed, due to new students' limited knowledge in the city of yogyakarta, we provide services that can be accessed online and a simple and efficient system. so that students do not need to come directly to the place, because it can be accessed online easily.

Visi Misi

  • To become the most complete and leading furniture company for boarding students in the region Yogyakarta City.
  • Become a furniture company that provides job opportunities for Public.
  • Become a furniture company with extensive partners.
  • Build good communication with consumers and implement customers membership program to have regular customers.
